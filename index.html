I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Oculus Research (aka Facebook Reality Labs). My primary research interests span robotics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.

I graduated in 2019 with a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Washington. At UW, I worked with Dieter Fox in the Robotics and State Estimation Lab. Before UW, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Computer Science from Duke University in 2012.
